# Cohort Retention & CLTV Analysis – Online Retail II

Analyzing customer repeat-purchase behavior for a UK online retailer.

**Goal:** Group customers by their first-purchase month, measure monthly retention, calculate Customer Lifetime Value, and recommend actions to reduce churn.

**Dataset:** Online Retail II – UCI ML Repository
https://archive.ics.uci.edu/dataset/502/online+retail+ii
Dec 2009 – Dec 2011, ~1.06M transactions


**Outputs:** Cohort retention heatmap, retention curves, CLTV by country, business recommendations.
